Legendary Broadcaster, Dick Vitale attends City of Palms Classic Fort Myers- On Thursday night, Andrew Wiggins, the number one recruit in the nation put on a show scoring 31 points at the City of Palms Classic. But Wiggings was not the biggest start inside Nevins Gymnasium, most eyes were on legendary broadcaster, Dick Vitale. Vitale, who has a home in Sarasota, says he loves watching high school games, and wasn't going to miss this year's City of Palms Classic, especially with Andrew Wiggins playing.
