Bishop Verot Gets Buzzer Beater to Break 37 Year Drought Fort Myers- The Bishop Verot Vikings snapped a 37 year losing streak at the City of Palms Classic and they did so in style. Trailing St. Thomas Aquinas 48-47 in the final seconds of the game, Max Williams hit a shot at the buzzer to give the Vikings the win, 49-48. It's Bishop Verot's first, first round win since 1975. Verot has been the host school since 1994. The win means the Vikings will face Montverde Academy (FL) in the second round on Wednesday.
