Montverde Academy wins 40th City of Palms Classic FORT MYERS- Kevin Boyle and Dakari Johnson celebrated a City of Palms Classic title two years ago with St. Patrick's. Last night, they won it again with Montverde Academy. Johnson had a double-double and took Tournament MVP honors as Montverde knocked off Utah's Lone Peak High School, 66-45, in the City of Palms Classic championship game. Montverde came into the game as the top ranked in the country in the ESPN poll, while Lone Peak was ranked first in the Max Preps poll. It's the first time a school from Florida has won the prestigious high school hoops tournament in 21 years. Posted: Dec 23, 2012 9:14 AM Updated: Dec 23, 2012 9:14 AM By Rishi Barran - email
