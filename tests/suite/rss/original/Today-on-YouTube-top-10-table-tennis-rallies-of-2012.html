Today on YouTube: top 10 table tennis rallies of 2012

There is nothing quite like a mega table tennis rally. So feast your eyes on this top 10 collective from the folk at Table Tennis Daily, who have compiled the best of 2012.

By Telegraph Sport

11:47AM GMT 27 Dec 2012

Comments

From Swedish great Jan-Ove Waldner to Timo Boll, there are some searing rallies here, not least the stunning diving winner from Britain's David Wetherill during the Paralympics.

On the face of it, there are even a few here from the amateur ranks, which leaves the rest of us yearning to play more in 2013 and replicate this set of shot-making brilliance.

And another thing...

Norwegian Havard Rugland earns NFL trial with New York Jets after posting spectacular kicking video.

AC Milan players attempt to sing Wham! classic ‘Last Christmas'.

Brazilian futsal star Falcao scores sublime backheel lob goal.

St Louis Rams wide receiver Danny Amendola celebrates a touchdown a little too enthusiastically.

Meet James Gallaugher, the Australian Usain Bolt – aged just 12-years-old

Santi Cazorla eat your heart out. Juventus striker Leonardo Bonucci shows what a dive is really supposed to look like.

Sociedad Sportiva Devoto forward Nicolas Barbero's goal against Almafuerte is so good referee Mariano Gonzalez shakes his hand.

Italian goalkeeper celebrates after the ball hits the crossbar during a penalty shoot-out. What happened next?

All Blacks fans amass in Trafalgar Square (courtesy of Sky Sports) to perform the traditional Haka war dance.

Lukas Podolski's stunning goal against Montpellier last week is very similar to one netted 42 years previously George Graham. Watch the two goals side be side in real time ...

Novak Djokovic scores a penalty before Fluminense versus Cruzeiro.

Neymar misses a penalty by miles in Brazil's game with Colombia.

Bangladesh debutant Sohag Gazi is welcomed to Test cricket by Chris Gayle who smashes 18 in the opening over.

NASCAR race ends prematurely after Jeff Gordon sends Clint Bowyer into the wall. A big scrap ensues.

Taison scores a volley for Metalist in their Europa League match against Rosenborg that Marco van Basten himself would have been proud of.

A missed field goal results in (possibly) the longest touchdown return in American football history.

Mr Mackey is disgusted by Lance Armstrong's chequered past in the latest episode of South Park.
